Top-Down Scrolling Shooter

This game is a top-down scrolling shooter where the player pilots a spaceship through space, dodging and destroying asteroids and alien ships. It’s a personal project I developed solo, using some visual assets from the Unity Asset Store, including alien ships, asteroids, and textures.


The objective is to survive and reach the end of the level while achieving the highest score possible. Points are earned by destroying enemies and avoiding collisions. I programmed all the gameplay mechanics, including a damage popup system—whenever the player hits an enemy, floating numbers appear and the enemy’s visible health bar decreases.
